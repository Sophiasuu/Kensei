## Design Context

### Users
Curious first-time seekers arriving on mobile or desktop to generate an instant multi-system spiritual reading. They want a fast path to personal insight without needing prior astrology knowledge.

### Brand Personality
Mystical, ceremonial, assured. The interface should feel like a guided rite rather than a generic wellness app.

### Aesthetic Direction
Flagship editorial redesign with a warm, tactile palette and a ceremonial sense of progression. Prioritize instant-reading conversion, dramatic hierarchy, and mobile-first clarity. Avoid generic AI mystic tropes, neon dark mode, glass cards, and interchangeable card grids.

### Design Principles
- Lead with ritual and trust, not dashboard tropes.
- Make the primary action obvious within seconds.
- Use typography, spacing, and motion to create ceremony without slowing the user down.
- Keep the reading experience immersive and guided, with strong section hierarchy and progressive disclosure.
- Preserve accessibility, touch comfort, and performance across mobile and desktop.
